Study for this week (Mar 29) 

The Romans Road

A simple path through Scripture explaining God’s plan of salvation.

1. Our Need for Salvation

Romans 3:10 — “None is righteous, no, not one.”

Romans 3:23 — “For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God.”

2. The Consequence of Sin

Romans 6:23 — “For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.”

3. God’s Provision Through Christ

Romans 5:8 — “But God shows His love for us in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us.”

Romans 5:12 — Sin entered the world through one man, and death through sin.

4. How to Receive Salvation

Romans 10:9–10 — Confess with your mouth, believe in your heart, and you will be saved.

Romans 10:13 — “Everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved.”

5. The Result of Salvation

Romans 5:1 — Justified by faith, we have peace with God.

Romans 8:1 — No condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus.

Want to know more? Open the Gospel of John and read chapter 3.
